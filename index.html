Hi there! I am Chandra Shekhar Murmu, a PhD student at the Department of Astronomy, Indian Institute of Technology Indore, and I am passionate about conducting research in Cosmology. Mainly I focus on the Intensity Mapping of the Universe, using bright line emission tracers from the galaxies such as the [C II]158μm, CO, [O III]88μm, etc. It also includes the [H I]21cm signal from the early IGM. This technique provides a relevant probe of the Epoch of Reionization, the era when the first luminous sources were formed and started illuminating our Universe!
